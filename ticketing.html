Ticketing

Tickets aren't for sale yet, check back later.

Youths

Youths will need tickets as well, but these will be available for ten bucks to anyone who has an adult ticket. For these purposes anyone 12 or younger is a youth - anyone older than that will need a full price ticket.

Resale

Tickets may only be resold for the face value or less, no exceptions.

Refunds

No refunds will be offered. If the event is cancelled due to weather / alien invasion / similar issues then existing tickets will be rolled over to the next year.
