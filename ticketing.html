Ticketing

50 tickets costing $80 apiece will go on sale at noon CDT on November 15. Each car will need a parking pass and we'll have 30 of those at ten bucks each.

Youths

Youths will need tickets as well, but these will be available for ten bucksto anyone who has an adult ticket. For these purposes anyone 12 or younger is a youth - anyone older than that will need a full price ticket.

Resale

Tickets may only be resold for the face value or less, no exceptions.

Refunds

No refunds will be offered. If the event is cancelled due to weather / alien invasion / similar issues then existing tickets will be rolled over to the next year.
